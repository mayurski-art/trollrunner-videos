# Mango Empire — Image Generation Prompts
### All 12 Scenes · Midjourney v6 / DALL-E 3 / Kling / Ideogram

Paste each **PROMPT** block directly into Midjourney or DALL-E.
For Midjourney append the **MJ PARAMS** line at the end of every prompt.
The **--no** (negative) list is critical — skip it and you'll get clipart heads
and floating fruit emoji instead of cinematic characters.

---

## Locked style suffix
Append this to the end of EVERY prompt before the params:

```
cinematic high-budget 3D animated crime thriller, dramatic chiaroscuro
lighting, photorealistic environment textures, shallow depth of field,
meme-noir atmosphere, Pixar-meets-crime-film render quality, vertical
portrait composition optimized for mobile, ultra-detailed --ar 9:16 --v 6
--style raw --q 2
```

## Universal negative prompt (add after every prompt in MJ as --no)
```
--no clipart, flat illustration, 2D cartoon, floating fruit emoji, human
faces, human skin, bald scalp, extra noses, text artifacts, blurry
characters, oversaturated colors, childish style, anime, sketch, watermark
```

## SCENE 1 — Balcony Empire

**Concept:** Mango Boss surveys his cartel island. Trollface reflected faintly
in the glass behind him — barely visible, always watching.

```
Anthropomorphic mango crime lord, glossy orange-yellow mango head with faint
natural dimpled skin texture, slicked-back hair detail on mango surface, smug
confident smirk, unbuttoned white silk shirt, gold chain necklace, gold watch,
black tailored trousers, holds a glass of whiskey — standing on a modern high-rise
penthouse balcony at blue-hour night. Below: a sprawling tropical cartel compound
seen from above — stacked colorful cargo containers, armed anthropomorphic fruit
guards patrolling neon-lit docks, glowing green and blue neon warehouse signs,
ocean waves beyond the port. Ocean wind subtly lifts the silk shirt collar.
Behind the mango boss, large floor-to-ceiling glass sliding doors reflect the
interior — and inside that reflection, barely discernible, a faint distorted
Trollface silhouette rendered as a ghostly black-and-white meme mask shape half-
dissolved into the glass reflection, watching from the darkness inside. The
reflection is subtle, not the focus — a detail for viewers who look closely.
Dramatic rim lighting from below catches the mango boss's face. Cinematic
foreground blur on the railing.
```

## SCENE 12 — The Reveal (FINALE)

**Concept:** Mango Boss laughs alone on the boat at night, unaware. Behind
him in the ocean fog, an enormous Trollface shadow rises — larger than the
boat. He was never the boss.

```
Wide cinematic shot from behind and slightly above: anthropomorphic mango crime
lord — glossy orange-yellow mango head, silk shirt open, arms spread wide —
standing at the stern of a speedboat cutting through dark ocean at night,
laughing freely, triumphant, totally unaware of what is behind him. The ocean
is dark, calm, shrouded in deep atmospheric fog banks. The boat is small in
the frame. From the fog directly behind the boat, rising out of the ocean
mist: an enormous towering Trollface shadow-form — not a physical character
but a massive silhouette the size of a building, formed from the shadows and
fog itself, condensing into the unmistakable shape of the classic Trollface
grin. It dwarfs the boat entirely. It is patient. It is watching the mango
boss, who has no idea it is there. This is the first time Trollface has been
shown fully — and it reveals he was always the scale of everything, always
behind everything. The Trollface shadow-form is fully formed and unmistakable
but made entirely of dark fog and negative space — not a character on the
boat but a presence emerging from the ocean itself. Moonlight from above
subtly traces the edges of the shadow shape. Epic scale contrast between the
tiny boat and the massive looming presence.
```
